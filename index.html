My Process

How I design.

Structured, yet human.

01
Discover & Research

Understand the user, the context, and the problem space through interviews, surveys, and competitive analysis before touching Figma.

02
Define & Frame

Synthesise research into user personas, journey maps, and a clear problem statement that guides every design decision.

03
Ideate & Wireframe

Explore multiple directions through low-fidelity sketches and wireframes. Speed over perfection at this stage — diverge first.

04
Design & Prototype

Build high-fidelity UI in Figma with a consistent design system, then prototype key flows for realistic user testing.

05
Test & Iterate

Run usability sessions, gather feedback, measure against success metrics, and refine. Design is never done — it evolves.
